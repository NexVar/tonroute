02 · The Idea

One guided flow — from idle TON to signed action.

TonRoute reads your wallet, proposes a strategy, shows the outcome before you commit, and routes the action through STON.fi and Tonstakers. Nothing moves until you sign.

Not a chatbot Not a DEX clone Not another widget Decision + explanation Real swap Real stake
03 · Product Flow

Four steps. One signature.

Each step is a real screen in the live product — not a mockup. Read-only on testnet by default.
STEP · 01
Connect
Wallet in.
TonConnect session restores automatically. Testnet-first. A built-in demo wallet covers the non-wallet jury.
STEP · 02
Analyze
Idle TON detected.
We read the live balance, split liquid TON from staked tsTON, and flag how much is actually sitting idle.
STEP · 03
Choose goal
Safe · Balanced · Yield.
One decision. The strategy engine explains why, previews the new allocation, and compares alternatives.
STEP · 04
Execute
Swap + stake, signed.
STON.fi swap when rebalancing is needed, then a Tonstakers stake — both in a single runtime flow you sign once.
04 · Strategy Engine

Three goals, one engine.

Rules are deterministic — every recommendation is reproducible and explainable. AI only narrates the reasoning; it never decides for the user.

Safe
30% stake · 70% liquid
Keeps most of the wallet liquid, puts only a light slice to work. For users who may need fast access.
tsTONLiquid TON
“Preserve optionality. Yield is a bonus, not the point.”
Balanced
60% stake · 40% liquid
Split between liquid TON and yield-bearing tsTON. Improves yield without giving up flexibility.
tsTONLiquid TON
“The default for most wallets — most of the time.”
Yield
85% stake · 15% liquid
Maximum staking participation with a thin gas/liquidity buffer. For holders who prioritize upside.
tsTONLiquid TON
“Put idle TON fully to work.”
05 · Integrations

Real actions. Not embeds.

STON.fi and Tonstakers are on the critical path of every recommendation. Swap and stake live inside the flow — not in a sidebar, not in a new tab.

STON.fiSwap layer

When the strategy needs rebalancing, we route the swap through STON.fi. It’s the action part of every mixed portfolio move.

TonstakersStake layer

TonConnectWallet layer
